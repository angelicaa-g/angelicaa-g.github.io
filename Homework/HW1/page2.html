The Three Types of AI (by Capabilities)

Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Narrow AI is the only kind of artificial intelligence that currently exists.
It is built to handle one specific task like translating languages, recognizing images,
or answering questions, and does it faster and more accurately than humans.
Examples include Siri, Alexa, and ChatGPT.

General AI (Strong AI)

General AI is still theoretical. It would be able to learn and apply knowledge across
different contexts, just like humans can. It could adapt, reason, and make decisions
without retraining.

Super AI

Super AI, or Artificial Superintelligence, is a futuristic idea where machines could
surpass human intelligence in every area. It could think, reason, and possibly feel
emotions or form goals of its own.

Source: IBM: Understanding the different types of artificial intelligence
