What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence, also known as AI, refers to computer systems that are able to think, learn, and solve problems in ways similar to humans.
These technologies can understand language, recognize patterns in data, and make useful predictions or suggestions to help people and industries.

How AI Works?

AI works by combining large amounts of data with powerful algorithms that allow machines to find connections, learn from examples,
and improve their performance over time. The more quality data an AI system processes, the smarter and more accurate it becomes.
